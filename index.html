Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins

Servings: 8 people

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Let it cool and enjoy!
